Questions

Things worth
asking.

When does it ship?

Early Bird I backers ship Q1 2027. Early Bird II and Standard ship Q1–Q2 2027. We'll send monthly updates — no silence.

How does it detect stress?

WOO monitors heart rate variability patterns — the rhythmic variation between heartbeats. Stress consistently changes these patterns in ways detectable 60–90 seconds before you feel it. This is established physiology, not a proprietary algorithm.

Does it need my phone nearby?

No. WOO is fully independent. Sensing, alerting, and guiding all happen on the ring itself. Sync to your phone only when you want to review patterns.

Will anyone know I'm wearing it?

No LED indicators. No visible sensors. No charging port visible when worn. It looks like a brushed titanium ring. That's the entire point.

What sizes are available?

US sizes 5 through 12. We'll send a sizing kit before manufacturing locks in — no guessing from a chart.

How is it different from Oura or RingConn?

Those rings track and report — they tell you what happened. WOO intervenes in the moment with guided breathing. Different category, different purpose. Also: no subscription.

What about my data and privacy?

All data is stored locally on the ring by default. Sync is optional. We don't see your data, sell your data, or need your data. Your health patterns are yours.

Is it a medical device?

No. WOO is a wellness product. It is not intended to diagnose, treat, or prevent any medical condition. If you have cardiovascular concerns, please consult a physician.

How accurate is the stress detection?

We're currently in validation with 200+ beta wearers. We'll share accuracy data before the campaign closes — we'd rather under-promise than disappoint.

What if the technology doesn't deliver?

Full refunds — no questions — if the core functionality doesn't work as described. We'll also communicate openly if timelines shift. Kickstarter backers accept risk; we accept responsibility for honesty.
